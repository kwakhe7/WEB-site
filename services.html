Our Services

Integrated mining and construction solutions tailored to your project.

Load & Haul

Efficient loading and hauling of overburden, ore, and waste across varied conditions with optimized fleet dispatch and cycle-time management.

Materials Handling

Stockpiling, rehandling, and feeding of processing plants with safe, controlled material flow and minimized spillage.

Dewatering

Pit and bench dewatering using pumps and boreholes to maintain safe working conditions and reduce downtime.

Drilling & Blasting

Production and presplit drilling with controlled blasting to achieve target fragmentation and slope stability.

Plant Hire

Flexible equipment rental from excavators and ADTs to dozers and graders, supplied with experienced operators.

Mine Planning

Short- to medium-term planning, scheduling, and reporting aligned to production targets and safety standards.

Cleaning of Slurry Ponds

Safe de-slurrying, rehabilitation, and maintenance of ponds to restore capacity and reduce environmental risk.

Rehabilitation

Landform shaping, topsoil placement, and revegetation to meet closure criteria and stakeholder expectations.

Crushing and Screening

On-site crushing, screening, and sizing to meet product specs with robust QA/QC controls.
